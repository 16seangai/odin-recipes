Neopolitan Pizza

Description

Neopolitan pizza features a thin, soft, and slightly chewy crust with a puffy, blistered edge, topped with vibrant tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella, fragrant fresh basil, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Italian flour
  • Salt
  • Active dry yeast
  • Water, room temperature
  • Tomatoes, crushed
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Make the dough by combining flour, salt, and yeast
  2. Add water gradually, mixing until a rough dough forms
  3. Knead by hand until smooth and elastic
  4. Cover and let rise for 18-24 hours until doubled and airy
  5. Divide the dough into 2 equal balls onto a floured surface
  6. Place each in a covered bowl and rest for 2 more hours
  7. Preheat oven to 500F for at least 45 minutes
  8. Shape each dough ball into 10-12 inch round
  9. Assemble the pizza by spreading a thin layer of crushed tomatoes, adding torn mozzarella, and topping with fresh basil and olive oil
  10. Bake on a preheated stone for 7-8 minutes
