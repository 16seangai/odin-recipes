Beef Chow Fun

Description

Beef Chow Fun is a classic Cantonese stir-fry dish featuring tender slices of beef tossed with wide, chewy rice noodles (called ho fun), crisp bean sprouts, and green onions, all coated in a savory soy-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fresh wide rice noodles
  • Thinly sliced flank steak or sirloin
  • Bean sprouts
  • Green onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef marinade with soy sauce, cornstarch, oil
  2. Loosen the noodles
  3. Make the stir-fry sauce by combining soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, white pepper
  4. Sear the beef over high heat
  5. Stir-fry the noodles
  6. Combine everything
