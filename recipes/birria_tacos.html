Birria Tacos

Description

Birria tacos are rich, crispy, cheesy tacos filled with tender, slow-cooked beef that's braised in a smoky, spiced chili sauce. The braising liquid becomes a flavorful consommé, which is served on the side for dipping. Originating from Jalisco, Mexico, birria tacos combine succulent shredded meat, melted cheese, and a satisfying crunch from frying the tortillas in the beef’s own juices.

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck roast
  • Dried guajillo chiles
  • Dried ancho chiles
  • Dried pasilla chiles
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Bay leaves
  • Cumin
  • Dried oregano
  • Paprika
  • Beef broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded Oaxaca cheese
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Prepare the chiles
  2. Make the braising sauce
  3. Cook the beef
  4. Shred the meat
  5. Assemble the tacos
